Project description. Provide at least one paragraph describing what your project is about:

Every pitchers in MLB are different in many ways. Some can throw a fastball at very high velocity, while others can command their pitch well. In this project, we will determine what makes a great pitcher in the MLB. Since there are many factors that can be considered for this topic, we will focus on two parts. The first part is the quality of each pitch types such as velocity and movements, and where they tend to throw it. In this part, we will also explore on how each pitchers uses different pitch types to determine what combinations of pitches make a pitcher more effective. The second part is the frequency of events such as walks and strikeouts for each pitcher. In order to differentiate between an effective pitcher and non effective pitcher, we will select several pitchers that performed well and several pitchers who performed poorly based on factors like WAR. Then we will compare the difference between them in terms of two parts that we explained earlier, to determine the traits of a good pitcher.

Project deliverables. Provide at least one paragraph describing what you plan to produce:

Through this, the user will see what locations make an effective pitcher.
